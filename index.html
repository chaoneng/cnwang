Charles C.N. Wang Ph.D

500, Lioufeng Rd., Wufeng, Taichung 41354, Taiwan · (04) 23323456#20053 · cnwang@asia.edu.tw

Dr. Charles C. N. Wang received his M.S. and Ph.D. degrees both in bioinformatics from Asia University, Taichung, Taiwan in 2008 and 2014. He is currently an associate professor with the department of bioinformatics, Asia University, Taichung, Taiwan. He is a data scientist with exhaustive experience in Bioinformatics, Semantic Computing, Natural Language Processing, and Machine Learning in several domains. He has published more than 50 academic research papers in various areas of semantic computing, text mining, natural language processing, and biomedical information. He is currently appointed as the section chief of development of AI Research Center and CEO of Center for Precision Medicine Research at Asia University. He is passionate about learning and always looks forward to solving challenging analytical problems.
